A macro close-up composition of a narrow gap between clusters of decorative rhinestones on a wall. Through the gap, a dense bundle of old electrical cables and wires is visible — frayed insulation in sick green and brown, corroded copper contacts, a slow drip of viscous grey-brown residue at the lowest point. The rhinestones surrounding the gap are impossibly bright and clean, catching pastel-pink light. The contrast between sparkle and rot is the entire subject. Palette: pastel pink and rhinestone sparkle dominant, sick greenish-grey wire insulation in the gap, dirty copper and grey-brown residue.
